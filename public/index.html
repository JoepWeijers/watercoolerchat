Watercooler chat ONLINE!

Have a random chat with your colleagues, as if you are standing by the watercooler!

How it works:

  • Decide on a moment with your colleagues to have a random chat, e.g. the typical coffee time 10am or 3pm
  • Browse to your company page at: https://watercoolerchat.online/company/COMPANYNAME/
  • Click the button to enter the queue.
  • We select a random colleague for you to chat with.
  • We'll show you both a unique link to a Talky chatroom.
  • Click the link, set up your devices, and start chatting!
